Training models for tooth wear evaluation

Our client gave us around 90 scans of sextants together with the labels. Hence, we can start to train our deep learning model. We already have a network architecture for our model which is using PointNet for 3D classification and segmentation. The model has been trained on 90 tooth models for 50 epochs with a learning rate of 0.001, using the Adam optimizer. Due to low amount of data, the accuracy obtained is quite low at about 20%-30%. We will continue to modify our deep learning model and our client also promise to provide us more data in the next two weeks. Hopefully by then the accuracy of our model will be improved. We choose to maintain the pre-trained model on the Azure virtual machine.
