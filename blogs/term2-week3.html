Discussion with project supervisor and teaching assistant about dataset issue

During our lab session this week, we discussed with Dr Yun Fu and our TA regarding the problem of dataset. Our client intended to provide us data and tooth models of real patients, however, the ethical approval process of using real patient data will take a very long amount of time. Hence, we are facing the problem of lack of dataset to train a deep learning model that we already have. After discussion with our TA, we are suggested to consider the option of machine learning and feature engineering since deep learning will not be feasible with such low amount of data that we currently have. When talking to Dr Yun Fu, we are told that feature engineering and machine learning might be very difficult for 3d models, we are suggested to change the project into analysing 2d tooth images or x-ray tooth scans since we only have about six weeks’ time left to finish up our work. While we are very surprised by this advice, Professor Dean appeared and told us that we could continue with what we are working on. He is satisfied with our elevator pitch last week and we should continue to work on the software. The main goal for this project should be to create a pipeline(software) that can take in 3d tooth models and view the tooth models, as well as pass the 3d images into an AI model and provide some results regarding the tooth models. The AI model doesn’t need to have high accuracy as this is just the first year of the project and we are building everything from scratch. Finally, we come to a conclusion that we will continue to work on the user interface and database of our software while waiting for our client’s response about how we should go on with the limited data.
