GDPR and Privacy of Data Statement

Our project deals with providing dentists with decision support in creating treatment plans. In this process we have used patient data to train our models with.

This data consists of 3D scans of the patient's teeth and personal data of the patient. The majority of the data gathered is not of real patients but consists of prosthetic teeth that have been modified and scanned accompanied with 'fake' personal data generated by dentists to fit the scans.

The small amount of real patient data includes 14 3D tooth models we have used has been accessed in compliance to the law through vendors who have made their data legally available for purchase and has been anonymised.

We are not responsible for any personal data that may be uploaded with the deployed version of our app as it is currently purely proof-of-concept and only usable for further development (not commercial use).
