Project Background

This is a project with Junaid Saleem Malik of the UCL Eastman Dental Institute. We were presented with the utility of clinical decision support systems to provide systematic and unified decision-making for treating dental disease. This has been explored and implemented quite widely within the field of prosthodontics. However, this technology has yet to break into the prosthodontic management of tooth wear due to the number of varying treatment approaches present. There are even several indeces to grade tooth wear.
