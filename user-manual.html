User Manual

Download and Run Wear3D

Note: This part must be done on a Windows laptop.

Step 1: Navigate to our github website using this link.

Step 2: Click on the Wear3D release on the right hand side.

Step 3: Click on the "executable.zip" file under Assets to download.

Step 4: When the download is completed, extract the zip file by right-clicking on executable.zip and select "Extract All..."

Step 5: Navigate into the "app" folder in the extracted "executable" folder and double-click "main.exe" to run Wear3D.

(Optional) Step 6: If the "Windows protected your PC" warning pop up when running the app, click on "More Info" as shown below:

(Optional) Step 7: Then click on "Run anyway" to run the application.

Log-in Page

This is your standard and simple login page to access the rest of the app, for those who are already registered. If not press the register button to make an account. (Register page explained below)

  1. Enter your email and password in the boxes indicated
  2. Then press sign in to sign in to continue to the home page

Register Page

  1. Enter a valid email and correct password. Dont forget to confirm your password.
  2. Then press register to create your account, then move onto sign in.

Home Page

  1. When you login you will first see the home page. On the left sidebar there will be a navigation panel to access all app pages.
  2. The main table on the home page is the database of all the patients. (You can click on a patients name to directly view their tooth scans).
  3. Click the add patient button to add a new patient. This will cause a sidebar on the right to appear.
  4. Here you can input all the patient details along with the patient tooth scans. Once you are done press `add patient`.
  5. Scroll with your mouse or using the scroll bar on the bottom to see the rest of the patient data.
  1. At the end of the patient table there is a delete button to remove a patient
  2. Make sure you want to delete the selected patient and confirm.

Viewing Page

  1. In the viewing page a patient's tooth scans can be seen and interacted with via using the cursor.
  2. The sider-bar on the right can be dragged left or right to minimise or maximise.
  3. The right side-bar contains basic functionailites such as temporarily hiding a specific scan, adding different scans manually, or resetting the viewpoint.

Results Page

  1. In the results page you simply enter a selected patient and press run analysis. You will see the prediction load in on top after some time.
  2. The bottom will display the treatment plan suggestion.
