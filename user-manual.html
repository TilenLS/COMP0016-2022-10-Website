User Manual

Log-in Page

This is your standard and simple login page to access the rest of the app, for those who are already registered. If not press the register button to make an account. (Register page explained below)

  1. Enter your email and password in the boxes indicated
  2. Then press sign in to sign in to continue to the home page

Register Page

  1. Enter a valid email and correct password. Dont forget to confirm your password.
  2. Then press register to create your account, then move onto sign in.

Home Page

  1. The main point of the home page is the data table with information of all the patients the dentist has access to. You can click on a patient name to directly see their tooth scans if they have them.
  2. On the left is the navigation side bar to access the rest of the page.
  3. Click the add patient button to bring up the side-bar on the right.
  4. Here a dentist can add patient data along with their tooth scans to add a new patient to the database.

Viewing Page

  1. In the viewing page a patient's tooth scans can be seen and interacted with via using the cursor.
  2. The sider-bar on the right can be dragged left or right to minimise or maximise.
  3. The right side-bar contains basic functionailites such as temporarily hiding a specific scan, adding different scans manually, or resetting the viewpoint.
